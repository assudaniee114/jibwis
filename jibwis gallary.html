IDAN KUNNE YA JI!!! Shugaban Majalisar Agajin Kaduna ta Arewa Mallam Umar Idris Gambo na jan Kunnen dukkan yan Agajin sa game da zaben gobe sati da cewa Haramun ne ko wa ni dan Agaji yasa wani abu a jikin sa da ke da alama ko alaka da kungiya duk girman sa duk kankancin sa. Duk wanda aka kama da taka wannan doka zai fuskanci hukunchi mai tsanani. M. Dabo Media & Publicity, Kaduna North Division. 3/9/21

ROHOTO: Majalisar Agajin Kaduna ta arewa karkashin jagorancin Shugaba Mallam Umar Idris Gambo ta gabatar da taro na bai daya a Gundumar Hayin Banki a jiya lahadi 6/6/21 an fara taro da misalin 4;35pm. Shugaba Gambo yayi bayanin tsarin gudanar da fareti da ke karamar Hukumar baki daya wanda aka dan mance, ya kuma ja hankali mahalarta taron sanar da duk abunda aka fadi a Wurin taro ga gundumomin su. Shugaba Gambo ya yaba matuka kan ayyukan Ramadan da ya gudana har Majalisar ta Karrama ma'aikatan makurar karamci da Qurani mai Girma. Shugaba Gambo ya maida Abdulaziz Umar Sanda Mukamin Assistant Divisional I.O saboda jajircewar sa Wurin aiki. Mallam Ishaq Abdullahi (Dep. State Traffic Officer) da Mallam Muhammad Sani Sufyan (State Commander) sun janyo hankulan yan Agaji kan bin doka da kiyaye aikata laifuka, domin in Gunduma tayi laifi karamar Hukuma za a Hukunta, Muhammad Sani Sufyan ya gargadi yan Agaji sa kaya da ke da Alaka da Sojoji. Rohoto: Idris Lawal. Hoto: Hamza Usman. Media & Publicity, FAG of JIBWIS Kaduna North Division, Kaduna State Directorate. 07/06/2021
